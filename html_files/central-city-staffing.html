Services Application Details

Services

During this 12-month program, you’ll gain training in a variety of areas as you help with the daily operations of Central City Concern’s housing buildings. Responsibilities include customer service, computer data entry, safety and security, janitorial services and emergency response.

Application Details

Before applying, you must:

  • Have a valid government-issued ID.
  • Have your Social Security card.
  • Be 90 days sober and prepared to pass a pre-employment drug test.

For questions, please contact CCStaffing@ccconcern.org.
