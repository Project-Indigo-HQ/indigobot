Biltmore Hotel

310 NW 6th Ave.
Portland, OR 97209

The Project-Based Section 8 SRO and waiting lists is currently open.

The Fair Market SRO waiting list is currently closed.

The Biltmore Hotel is a three-story apartment building located in the Old Town neighborhood of downtown Portland. All units are single-room occupancy with shared bathroom facilities. Most units are rent subsidized through project-based Section 8, with residents paying a portion of the rent.

Community Overview

  • Number of units: 76
  • Built: 1905
  • Public transportation: Centrally located in downtown Portland. Multiple bus/MAX routes available in walking distance.
  • Grocery stores: Old Town Grocery (0.3 miles), Safeway (0.9 miles), Whole Foods Market (0.4 miles)
  • Parks: North Park Blocks (0.1 mile), Tom McCall Waterfront Park (0.5 mile)
  • Health facilities: Old Town Clinic (0.2 mile), Old Town Recovery Center (0.2 mile), Providence Medical Facility (1.2 miles), OHSU Hospital (2.5 miles)
  • Other: Portland Farmers Market at Portland State University (0.8 mile)

The Biltmore Hotel PBV Section 8  waiting list is currently open.

To apply, first review the screening and eligibility requirements below, found in Tenant Selection Plan (Section 8) and Building Criteria (Fair Market).  Then, submit a pre-application for the unit type you are interested in:

The Biltmore Hotel Section 8 Tenant Selection Plan

  • If you are interested in rent-subsidized units at the Biltmore Hotel, submit the Section 8 pre-application following the instructions in the section below
  • Preference form  Preference Form Biltmore

The Biltmore Hotel Fair Market Building Criteria

  • The Biltmore Hotel Fair Market waitlist is currently closed.  Pre-application materials can be found here when the waitlist is open.

Submitting Your Section 8 Pre-Application Materials:

  • Email. Send your completed and signed pre-application materials to biltmoreapply@ccconcern.org for Section 8 units.
  • In-person. Bring your completed and signed pre-application materials to the CCC Housing Office at 523 NW Everett St., Portland OR, 97209.

Note: CCC strongly encourages applicants to submit pre-application forms by email so there is a electronic record and we can provide a receipt. CCC will not accept faxed applications or those submitted directly at the Biltmore Hotel.

Amenities

  • Laundry
  • Community room
  • Smoking/non-smoking
  • Wheelchair accessible
  • On-site services
  • On-site staff

Pets are not allowed at the Biltmore Hotel.  Applicants/Tenants may request service or assistance animals as a reasonable accommodation when necessary to support a verified disability. All requests are subject to management approval.

Eligibility and screening

  • A security deposit is not required at the Biltmore.
  • For more information on screening requirements, refer to the Biltmore Tenant Selection Plan and Building Criteria.

Units at the Biltmore are income and rent restricted based on a percentage of Median Family Income (%MFI) for Multnomah County. Households must earn annual income below the corresponding %MFI to be eligible for the unit. Click here for information to help you determine the maximum income limit.
