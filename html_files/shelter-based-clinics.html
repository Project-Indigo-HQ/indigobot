Shelter-Based Clinics

Hansen Clinic at the Banfield Motel Shelter
1525 NE 37th Ave., Room 38

503-329-6460

The Hansen Clinic provides supportive primary care for the 60 CCC clients staying at the Banfield shelter. We help clients manage their health, avoid emergency room visits and access primary care.

Services:

  • Treatment for addiction, including medication-supported recovery.
  • Help getting prescription refills for chronic conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure and COPD.
  • Connections to ongoing primary care.

 

We can treat:

  • Skin conditions, including cuts, wounds, bites, rashes and infections.
  • Acute injury or infection.
  • Respiratory illness.
  • Fever.
  • Nausea.
  • Vomiting.
  • Diarrhea.

How to access care

The Hansen Clinic is open only to individuals staying at the Banfield Motel Shelter.

Insurance information:

We don’t turn anyone away for lack of insurance or inability to pay. We accept Oregon Health Plan, Medicare, self-pay and some private insurance. Need coverage? We can help you get set up.

COVID-19 safety:

Our clinics are clean and safe for in-person visits with your provider. Learn more about our response to the pandemic.
