Clean Start

Established in 1996, Clean Start is a homeless-to-work employment program offering consistent work opportunities while giving back to the community and addressing Portland’s pressing issues of street cleanliness and homelessness recovery. Throughout the six-month program, participants receive job training and mentorship while removing graffiti, trash, needles, and biohazards throughout the Portland metro area, partnering with Downtown Clean & Safe to fill cleaning requests in the downtown core.

Services Begin Your Journey

Services

Over six months, trainees in the program receive peer mentoring while learning critical job skills, helping build confidence for future employment opportunities. Trainees are also connected with CCC’s wraparound services, including:

  • Supportive housing
  • Health care
  • Substance use disorder treatment
  • Employment assistance

Most trainees exit the program with long-term employment and housing!

Begin Your Journey with Clean Start

Gain new skills while keeping Portland neighborhoods clean with Clean Start! Before applying, you must:

  • Have a valid government-issued ID
  • Have your Social Security card
  • Be sober and prepared to pass a pre-employment drug test
